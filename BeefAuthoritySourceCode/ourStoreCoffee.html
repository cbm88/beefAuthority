Description Extended Information
 
Primal Feast is produced using enzymatic and thermal (high temperature/pressure) processing. This process is gentler and more time consuming than our competitors manufacturing processes and more expensive. The result, however, is a smaller protein molecule which not only enhances bioavailability but produces a base powder that has a less acidic taste and is easier to acceptable flavor. In other words—Primal Feast has a head start on great taste built into the ingredient manufacturing process!

Because our source material is meaty bones (not skins, ears, noses and hooves our complete protein amino acid profile is superior and yet also high in the naturally occurring amino acids found in connective tissue glycine and proline and other collagen peptides for functionalities like joint care, digestion, muscle recovery.
